ABOUT ME

A bit of background on who I am

My name is Tyler Pokoski, currently working at the USDA-Agricultural Research Service as an Engineering Technician in Fort Collins, CO. Most of my work regards assisting in studies regarding water use management and monitoring in agriculture, most specifically with corn. I spend most of my time in the fields collecting data from various sensors (hence my only 'professional' photo being from my wedding), I do also work in the office with managing data and mapping UAV data of the study fields. More specifics of my research and work experience can be found in my resume.

Currently I am a student at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, enrolled in the Online GIS Development Master's Program. Just finishing up my first few classes, and having a wonderful time so far!

In my personal life, I enjoy the outdoors via hiking and biking. On rainy days you can catch me playing board games or painting miniatures for the aforementioned games.
