The Trans-Siberian Bikeway

For the second lab assignment in GEOG 572, I was tasked with designing a 'Tour de France' of another country. The country I picked was Mongolia, and it features a starting point in Siberia, then ending in the capitol of Mongolia. This lab gave me a lot of experimentation with creating hillshades and photoshop experience.
